LS13 Server Rules

      0.   Don’t be a dick.

  1. Metagaming is strictly prohibited.
  1. Metagaming is the sharing of In-character information through any Out of Character channel.
  1. Erotic Roleplay is strictly prohibited.
  2. Any attempt to exploit or otherwise inhibit or modify the proper function of the game server is strictly prohibited.
  3. Removing a player from a round through either restraint, destruction, or otherwise may only be done with sufficient IC justification.

In-Character Policies

  1. This server operates under a “Light RP” goal. While you are expected to remain in character, you are generally free to play as you wish as long as you obey the policies and rules of the server.
  2. Heads of staff, members of the security team, and silicon lifeforms such as the AI and Cyborgs are held to a higher standard and expected to fulfill their job-related duties.
  1. Security is expected to respect space law procedures, punishment guidelines, and other requirements.
  2. Exceptions are made for other, overriding IC obligations such as being an Antagonist, or Golem who must obey his master’s orders.
  1. Player controlled Flora, Fauna, and Artifical life not bound by laws are not antagonists by default, and are expected to follow the same rules as normal crewmembers.
